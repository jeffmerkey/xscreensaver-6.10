
So this works ok:

swayidle -w \
  timeout 60 "xscreensaver-wayland.sh --verbose --start" \
  resume     "xscreensaver-wayland.sh --verbose --stop"

this will activate swaylock and lock the console.  It may not 
always work properly on all compositors:

swayidle -w \
  timeout 60 "xscreensaver-wayland.sh --verbose --start" \
  resume     "xscreensaver-wayland.sh --verbose --stop" \
  timeout 90 'swaylock -f'

"xscreensaver-command --cycle" seems to work.
